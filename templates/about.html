About the page

Payroller is the web page where the users can receive detailed information about the taxes and other related contributions to the state budget.



The treasure transactions, mostly, are made by the employers on behalf of the employees. As a result, the employees do not have enough information concerning the amounts they contribute to financing state authorities and are less demanding of state governance.



In the second stage of Payroller's development, users will be able to create loan amortization tables.



By the final stage of the web portal's development, users will be able to have their authorization data and control their monthly payroll by saving the previous data. Moreover, companies will be able to make payroll calculations for all their employees, see the details of accruals and deductions, prepare the payslips and provide these to their employees.
